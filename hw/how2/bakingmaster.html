How to Bake Cupcakes

Let's Get One Thing Straight

If you're here, its because you don't know how to bake for shit and you want to learn. I'm going to start off simple, because if you can't even get this, then you're a lost cause. Whoever you are, you are in the right place.

Step 1: Gather Ingredients

  • 1 box of cupcake mix (choose your favorite brand)
  • x eggs
  • x water
  • x oil
  • x chocolate chips (optional)
  • tub of frosting (I will be using Duncan Hines' Cream Cheese Icing)
  • 12 muffin/cupcake liners

Step 2: Setting Up

  1. Preheat the oven to x degrees (always do this first so while you mix everything the oven can warm up)
  2. Grab your cupcake pan and place one liner in each spot
  3. Collect all of your ingredients, measuring cups, and bowls and place them in one area on the counter

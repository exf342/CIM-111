Let's Get One Thing Straight

If you're here, its because you don't know how to bake for shit and you want to learn. I'm going to start off simple, because if you can't even get this, then you're a lost cause. Whoever you are, you are in the right place.

Step 1: Gather Ingredients

  • 1 box of cupcake mix
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable/ canola oil
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)
  • 1 tub of frosting
  • 24 cupcake liners

Step 2: Setting Up

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees (always do this first so while you mix everything the oven can warm up)
  2. Grab your cupcake pan and place one liner in each spot
  3. Collect all of your ingredients, measuring cups, and bowls and place them in one area on the counter

Step 3: Mix Ingredients

  • Mix cake mix, water, oil and eggs in a large bowl until lumps are gone
  • Add chocolate chips if desired

Step 4: Pour Batter

Pour batter into individual liners. Only fill it about 2/3 of the way up.

Step 5: Bake Cupcakes

  1. Insert tray into oven and bake 12-17 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cupcake comes out clean
  2. Remove from oven and allow them to cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan
  3. Turn off the oven (key step right here)
  4. After cooling completely, you can frost

Step 6: Frost and Enjoy

Nubrella Manifesto

There are many things in life that will let you down: gravity, elevators, people,
and most importantly the weather.
For many, this can complicate a day.
We don’t want you to slow down.
Mother nature is outside your control but being prepared isn’t.
We are there to prepare you. To protect you.
To allow you to reach your full potential.
Time doesn’t slow down, life doesn’t stop, and work doesn’t pause.
People have a lot to do and not a lot of time to do it.
There are only so many hours in a day and all 24 of them should be un-limited.
We are go-getters.
The first ones in and the last ones out.
We love life too much to let anything slow us down.
We are Nubrella.
